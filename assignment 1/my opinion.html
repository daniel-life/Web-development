my opinion on CSGO

In my opinion, CSGO is a great game for FPS fanatics like me. It is a highly detailed with good mechanics and tactical skills
with a great map designs and addictive game modes for all ages. For those who are playing with a friend, you can try wingman while
for those more serious gamers who want to progress up, the competitive mode is one of the best modes in this game.
Finally, the community market encourages growth of CSGO economy for valve and attracts people to play and trade with other players.

downsides of CSGO

Not every game can be perfect and CSGO is no exception. Some servers may be unstable and as a result gameplay experience may be badly affected.
Also, it is not a easy game for beginners to learn, it takes time,dedication and skills to be good at this game.
Finally, players can spend way more money than expected due to cases and keys to get those beautiful skin. As a result, scams may occur and players may lose alot of money is not careful, especially on gambling sites.
